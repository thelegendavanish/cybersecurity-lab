The Toll of Time Theft

🐢

QoS Degradation

Unauthorized usage eats into the Quality of Service. Bandwidth caps are reached faster, causing slow speeds for legitimate users.

💸

Economic Drain

In many regions, internet is sold by time or volume. Stealing time is a direct financial theft from the account holder.

⚖️

Legal Violation

Unauthorized access to computer networks (WLAN) is illegal under IT laws and can lead to heavy fines or imprisonment.



Securing Your Connection

🔑

WPA3 Encryption

Ensure your router uses the latest encryption standards. Avoid using WEP or WPA as they are easily cracked.

🚫

MAC Filtering

Configure your network to only allow specific physical device addresses (MACs) to connect, blocking strangers.

📶

Disable SSID Broadcast

Hiding your network name makes it harder for casual wardrivers to find and target your connection.
