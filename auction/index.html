Common Auction Scams

🤖

Shill Bidding

Sellers or their bots bid on their own items to artificially drive up the price and create fake demand.

📦

The "Non-Delivery"

The seller receives payment but never ships the item, or ships something completely different (like a brick in a box).

🛑

Off-Platform Escrow

Scammers lure buyers away from the platform's secure payment system to use non-reversible wire transfers.



Protecting Your Bids

🛡️

Use Official Escrow

Never pay outside the auction site. Official systems hold money until you confirm the item is received.

📊

Check Seller History

Detailed feedback and account age are critical. Zero-review accounts selling high-value items are a major red flag.

🧐

Analyze Bidding Patterns

If the same user bids immediately after you every single time, it's likely a shill bot driving up your price.
