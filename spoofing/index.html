The "Triple Shield" of Email

📄

SPF

Sender Policy Framework: A DNS record that lists which IP addresses are allowed to send email for your domain.

🔏

DKIM

DomainKeys Identified Mail: Adds a digital signature to emails, allowing the receiver to verify the content hasn't been tampered with.

🚦

DMARC

Domain-based Message Authentication: Tells the receiver what to do if SPF or DKIM fails (Block, Quarantine, or Allow).



How to Spot Spoofing

🔍

Check Headers

Always inspect the "Return-Path" or "Authentication-Results" headers to see the true origin.

🤖

MTA-STS

Modern protocols that force encrypted and authenticated communication between mail servers.

🔒

BIMI

Displays verified brand logos in the inbox so users can visually confirm identity.
