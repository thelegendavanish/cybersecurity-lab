The Art of Deception

📜

Physical Forgery

Altering handwritten documents, cheque numbers, or paper currency. Requires physical chemical removal of ink and matching paper grain.

💻

Digital Forgery

Using software like Photoshop to alter PDFs, certificates, or flight tickets. Often detected via metadata analysis and pixel inconsistencies.

🤖

Deepfake Era

The modern frontier of counterfeiting: replicating human voices and faces using AI to commit high-value wire fraud and disinformation.



Forensic Detection

🔎

Level 1: Visual

Checking for intaglio printing (raised ink), watermarks, and security threads under transmitted light.

🔦

Level 2: Forensic

Using UV lights, infrared scanners, and video spectral comparators (VSC) to reveal hidden security data.

💾

Level 3: Digital

Cryptographic signatures and blockchain verification for e-documents, ensuring the original hash remains unchanged.
