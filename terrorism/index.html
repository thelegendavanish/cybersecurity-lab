Critical Infrastructure Defense

🧱

Air Gapping

Physically isolating critical control networks from the public internet to prevent remote access.

🧠

Anomaly Detection

AI systems that monitor "normal" electrical patterns and block any commands that look like sabotage.

🔄

Redundancy

Mechanical fail-safes that don't rely on software to prevent physical damage if the system is hacked.
