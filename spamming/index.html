The Cost of Spam

📉

Productivity Loss

Employees spend an average of 15-20 minutes a day sorting through spam, costing billions in lost time.

🔌

Server Overload

Spam accounts for ~45% of all email traffic, straining global network bandwidth and storage.

🎣

Delivery for Malware

Spam is the primary delivery vehicle for phishing links, ransomware, and credential harvesters.



Filter Mechanics

🔡

Keyword Analysis

Filters look for "trigger words" like 'Free', 'Act Now', or excessive punctuation and caps.

🕵️

Bayesian Filtering

Statistical learning that calculates the probability of a message being spam based on past examples.

📜

Blacklists (RBL)

Real-time databases of IP addresses known for sending large volumes of unsolicited mail.
