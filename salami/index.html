The "Salami Slicing" Concept

🔪

The Technique

Attackers program the system to shave off exactly ₹1 from every transaction.

📈

Massive Scale

While one rupee seems small, doing this across millions of accounts every day results in huge sums of money over time.

🕵️

Hard to Detect

Individual account holders rarely notice a missing ₹1 in a large transaction, and the bank's books often seem to balance on a macro level.



Mitigating Salami Attacks

📏

Strict Rounding Rules

Implementing standardized IEEE-754 rounding across all financial modules to prevent custom "shaving" logic.

🔬

Precision Auditing

Continuously comparing total inputs with total outputs down to four or more decimal places.

🤖

AI Anomaly Detection

Detecting infinitesimal patterns of leakages that deviate from mathematical probability.
